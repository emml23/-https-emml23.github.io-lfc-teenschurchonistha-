More About Living Faith Church

Living Faith Church (LFC), founded by Bishop David Oyedepo in 1983, is one of the largest Pentecostal churches globally, headquartered in Nigeria. The church emphasizes faith, prosperity, healing, and supernatural teachings.

The LFC Teens Ministry is dedicated to mentoring young people, encouraging strong biblical foundations, spiritual gifts, and community impact. It aims to raise a generation rooted in Christ and equipped to lead in faith and service.

Through dynamic programs, outreach events, and education, the ministry nurtures youth in character, wisdom, and purpose.
