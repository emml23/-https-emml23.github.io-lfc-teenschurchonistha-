Weekly Programs

  • Sunday Services – 7:00 AM & 9:00 AM Join us every Sunday morning for two inspiring services. The 7:00 AM service kicks off with heartfelt worship, empowering sermons, and prayer, setting a strong spiritual tone for the week. The 9:00 AM service offers an extended session with special teen-focused worship, group discussions, and mentorship, designed to nurture faith and leadership in our youth. Both services welcome everyone to experience renewal and community fellowship.
  • Wednesday Mid-week Service – 4:30 PM Our midweek gathering is designed to deepen your spiritual walk through engaging Bible studies and open discussions. This is a great time for teens to connect, ask questions, and grow in understanding. The interactive format encourages participation and practical application in everyday life.
  • Monday Choir Practice – 5:00 PM Elevate your worship experience by joining our vibrant choir! Practice is open to teens interested in developing their vocal talents while building close friendships. Music ministry is a key part of our church culture, and we welcome all skill levels to come and be a part of this joyous expression.
  • Friday Youth Fellowship – 6:00 PM Friday evenings bring youth together for fellowship, fun activities, games, and Bible-based workshops. This program fosters strong friendships, mentorship opportunities, and encourages teens to lead positive change both inside and outside the church.
  • Monthly Community Outreach Every month, our teens actively participate in community service projects such as food drives, neighborhood clean-ups, and visits to orphanages. This initiative helps develop compassion, leadership, and a strong sense of purpose aligned with Christian values.
